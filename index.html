ABOUT PELICULA

Pelicula, film festival of NSS College of Engineering, Palakkad, is a premier event that celebrates the magic of cinema, showcasing a diverse range of films that inspire, educate, and entertain. This exciting event brings together film enthusiasts, students, and industry professionals to share their passion for storytelling, creativity, and innovation, fostering a dynamic community that appreciates the art of filmmaking.
